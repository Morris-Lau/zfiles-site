About

ZFiles helps you browse local files, hidden files and folders, SMB shares, and SFTP servers that you configure yourself. The full app is free to try for 7 days, then ZFiles Pro unlocks continued access with a one-time purchase.

The app has no ads, no subscriptions, no analytics, and no tracking.
